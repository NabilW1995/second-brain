# Second Deal

The **second deal** is an advanced card sleight where you appear to deal the top card, but actually deal the second card from the top — keeping the top card in place.

## Learning Resources

### Videos
- [Second Deal Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wqzLQFjScTs)

## Key Points
- Used to control which card is dealt while appearing natural
- Two main grips: push-off second deal and strike second deal
- Requires extensive practice for smooth, silent execution
- The "take" should look identical to a normal deal
- Sound is a big tell — practice until silent

## Applications
- Cheating at cards (historically)
- Magic tricks requiring card control
- Gambling demonstrations
