# Pinky Break

The **pinky break** is a fundamental card sleight used to secretly hold a separation in the deck. It's essential for many tricks including double lifts, passes, and controls.

## Learning Resources

### Videos
- [Pinky Break Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NPMifa5p4bU)

## Key Points
- Hold a tiny gap with the tip of your pinky at the inner edge of the deck
- Should be invisible from the front — angles matter
- Relaxed hand position is key (tension = visible)
- Used to track cards, prepare for lifts, or set up for passes
