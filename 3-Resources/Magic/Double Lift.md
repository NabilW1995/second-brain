# Double Lift

The **double lift** is one of the most fundamental and essential sleight-of-hand moves in card magic. It involves lifting two cards from the top of the deck while making it appear as though only one card is being shown.

## Learning Resources

### Videos
- [Double Lift Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Gjd4EDm3EWU)

## Key Points
- Practice getting a consistent "break" under the top two cards
- The lift should look natural and identical to lifting a single card
- Speed comes from smoothness, not rushing
- Many variations exist (push-off, strike, pinky count, etc.)
